Privacy Policy for Lagom

Effective Date: 21 April 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how Lagom (“App”) developed by Hoomans Co. handles your information.

1. What We Collect

Lagom helps users manage their screen time and related usage behaviors. The App collects:

  • App usage statistics
  • Screen time and daily usage summaries
  • Sleep tracking (if enabled)
  • Permissions to block apps

All data is stored locally on your device. No personal data is collected, transmitted, or stored on our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to improve the functionality and reliability of Lagom:

  • Mixpanel – For anonymous usage analytics, strictly without any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Firebase Crashlytics – For crash reporting and diagnostics. No personal data is shared.

These services comply with their own privacy policies. You can review them here:

  • Mixpanel Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy

3. No Login Required

Lagom does not require users to create an account or log in. Your use of the App remains anonymous.

4. Subscriptions

Lagom offers paid subscriptions via Apple’s in-app purchase system. We do not collect payment information directly. Subscription management and cancellation are handled through your Apple account.

5. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, we do not store any user data that would require deletion or export upon request.

6. Children’s Privacy

Lagom is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children. If you believe a child has used the App or shared personal information with us, please contact us at dev@hoomans.dev, and we will take steps to delete any associated data.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at: dev@hoomans.dev
